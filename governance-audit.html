AI Governance & Autonomous Systems Audit

Make your autonomous AI trustworthy, auditable, and regulatory-ready

The Problem

You're deploying autonomous AI systems in production. Your governance frameworks look solid internally. But when regulators ask how decisions are documented, how overrides are tracked, and what your audit trail actually proves—most companies can't confidently answer.

The Future of Life Institute's 2025 AI Safety Report found that most companies failed safety preparedness reviews. The gap isn't usually intentions—it's governance execution.

Our Approach

We don't consult theoretically. We've built autonomous AI in real business operations (vending systems, shipping operations, merchant services). We've stress-tested governance frameworks under pressure and learned what actually works.

We audit your systems from the perspective of someone who's built and deployed these systems, not someone reading about them.

What We Audit

  • Decision Documentation: How are autonomous decisions logged, explained, and justified?
  • Audit Trails: Can you prove what happened, when, and why? Will that hold up to regulatory review?
  • Override Systems: When humans override autonomous decisions, is it documented and traceable?
  • Framework Coherence: Do your governance systems actually enforce what you claim they do?
  • Regulatory Readiness: Would your documentation pass an auditor's scrutiny?
  • Gap Identification: Where are the governance weaknesses? What needs to change?
